# Menopause Support App for Nigerian Women

## What the Product Does
Never Alone supports Nigerian women aged 40 to 50 as they navigate menopause by providing personalized education, symptom tracking, mental health support, and culturally relevant community engagement.

## Why It Matters
Many Nigerian women face stigma, low awareness, and lack of healthcare access related to menopause. This app helps bridge these gaps, improving their health outcomes and quality of life.

## Who It's For
Women in Nigeria within the menopausal age range, especially those with limited access to information and healthcare, along with their caregivers and health professionals.

## Key Features & Technologies
- Personalized symptom tracking and health advice tailored to Nigerian demographics.
- Myth-busting educational content in local languages.
- Moderated community forums for peer support.
- Offline capabilities and SMS notifications for low connectivity users.
- Built with React Native, Node.js, and MongoDB.
